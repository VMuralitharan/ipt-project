Top Attractions

Sigiriya Rock Fortress

A UNESCO World Heritage Site, this ancient rock fortress offers breathtaking views and historical insights.

Ella

A beautiful mountain town perfect for hiking, sightseeing, and enjoying lush greenery and waterfalls.

Galle Fort

Walk through colonial streets, enjoy ocean views, and explore charming boutiques in this historic Dutch fort.

Upcoming Events

  • April 10: Sinhala and Tamil New Year Celebrations – All across Sri Lanka
  • May 15: Vesak Festival – Religious lantern displays and cultural shows
  • June 22: Kandy Esala Perahera – Traditional parade with dancers and elephants

Recommended Accommodations

  • Jetwing Lighthouse, Galle: Luxury stay with a view of the Indian Ocean.
  • 98 Acres Resort, Ella: Eco-friendly retreat surrounded by tea plantations.
  • Cinnamon Grand, Colombo: Ideal for comfort, shopping, and city exploration.

Travel Tips

  • Carry light clothing for the tropical climate.
  • Respect local customs, especially at religious sites.
  • Use bottled water for drinking.
  • Public transport is cheap, but tuk-tuks are best for short trips.

Getting Around

Sri Lanka has various transport options:

  • Trains: Scenic rides, especially from Kandy to Ella.
  • Buses: Affordable and widely available across the country.
  • Taxis & Tuk-tuks: Great for short trips; always agree on the price beforehand.
  • Car Rentals: Available in major cities and airports for flexible travel.

Must-Try Dishes

  • Kottu Roti: A spicy street food made from chopped roti, vegetables, and meat.
  • Hoppers: Bowl-shaped pancakes served with egg or coconut sambol.
  • Pol Sambol: A spicy coconut mix served with rice and curry.
  • Milk Tea: The national drink – sweet, milky and refreshing!

Where to Shop

  • Pettah Market (Colombo): Bustling market with local goods and textiles.
  • Laksala: Government-owned store with authentic souvenirs and crafts.
  • Odel / Crescat Boulevard: Trendy malls with local and international brands.
